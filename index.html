Xiaomin Ouyang

I am a last-year PhD student at the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Guoliang Xing and Prof. Jianwei Huang. I also work closely with Prof. Jiayu Zhou.

My research interests include Artificial Intelligence for Internet of Things, Mobile Computing and Networked/Distributed Systems, with a primary focus on developing novel machine learning systems and sensing technologies for smart health applications. I am currently working on the design and deployment of a multimodal federated learning system for Alzheimer’s Disease monitoring.

I obtained my bachelor’s degree with honor (rank 1/68) in 2019 from Information and Communication Engineering, Xiamen University.
